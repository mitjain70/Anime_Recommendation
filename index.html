My Absolute Must-Watch Picks

1. Death Note

The perfect "starter" anime. It's a dark, smart thriller that's more like a crime show. A high school genius finds a notebook that can kill anyone whose name is written in it. Pure suspense.

2. One Piece

The ultimate adventure. Yes, it has 1000+ episodes, but there's a reason. The world is massive, the crew feels like family, and the story just gets better and better.
